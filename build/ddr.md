## 3. Probable Root Cause
- Gaps in tile joints allowing moisture ingress.
- Concealed plumbing issues reported.
- External wall cracks allowing rainwater ingress.

## 4. Severity Assessment
**Severity: Medium to High**

Multiple areas affected with prolonged moisture exposure.
## 5. Recommended Actions
- Repair tile joints in wet areas.
- Inspect and repair plumbing lines.
- Seal external wall cracks.
- Repair plaster and repaint after drying.

## 6. Additional Notes
- Thermal readings support moisture presence.
- Observations limited to visible areas.
